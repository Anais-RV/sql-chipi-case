SI ALGO FALLA


 "FATAL: role 'postgres' does not exist"
    PostgreSQL no instalado. Descarga: postgresql.org

 "psql: command not found"
    Añade a PATH: C:\Program Files\PostgreSQL\16\bin

 "password authentication failed"
    Ejecuta: $env:PGPASSWORD="postgres"

 "database chipi_case already exists"
    Ejecuta: make reset
